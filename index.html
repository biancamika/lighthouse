LIGHTHOUSE

Don't be kept in the dark.

In this current age of technology, secrets are now easier to keep. Even your loved ones aren't exceptions. With measures such as passwords and alerts, how will you ever know of such acts? Lighthouse is a security software that uncovers anything hidden in people's gadgets, from messages to private accounts. Nothing will stay hidden from you ever again.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

After connecting to gadgets of your choice, you can make use of these functions.

Find secrets.

When you turn on Enlighten, all that stays unseen is revealed on the screen. This way, you don't have to go checking everything for changes.

Get alerts.

Be notified of any new changes, whether it's a new account or contact number.

Stay secure.

HOW DO I START?

See the two buttons below ('Enlighten' and 'Darken')? They simulate the effect of the Lighthouse software on gadgets. Try testing them with the example below.
